amendment to appendix i of the constitution of the european commission for the control of foot-and-mouth diseasesource: the european commission for the control of foot-and-mouth disease at its fifth session held in rome on 17 and 18 april 1958 decided, in conformity with the provisions of article xiv of the constitution, to amend the scale of contributions appearing in the said appendix by reducing the contribution of greece from $1,500 to $750. the amendment came into force on 31 october 1958, the date of approval by the council of the food and agriculture organization of the united nations, in accordance with article xiv of the constitution.
